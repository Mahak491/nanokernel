Delivering the Finest Information Technology Solutions and Industrial Automation Services with over 25 Years of Expertise.

NANO KERNEL Ltd., (NKL), a core technology company, based in Bangalore, was founded by a group of professionals in 1997, with a passion to contribute their capabilities and also capitalize on the growing opportunities in the Information Technology Arena. NKL provides a wide range of Web-based IT Landscape solutions to a whole array of industries along with its e-com services.

NKL professionals have strong experience in client/server environments, web-enabled application development, corporate web and portal development, and have developed many Web Applications as well as Web-Based Products.

During the course, NKL established the Industrial Automation Division to address the potential needs of the manufacturing industries. By bringing in the necessary knowledge and entering into long-term agreements with the companies that provided support, NKL created a strong team that is now capable of taking projects of any size. This could range from a simple material handling system to a fully integrated Robotics and AI system. For any need in industrial automation, NKL can be viewed as a one-stop shop.

Transforming Industries with Innovative Software Solutions and Industrial Automation Excellence

NKL has a software development division, equipped with an excellent infrastructure and dedicated team with international standards. NKL design and maintain high-quality software with multi-platform capabilities.

With considerable expertise in Software Development and System Integration, NKL undertakes turnkey projects.
